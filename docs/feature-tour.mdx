## How publishing works - small change

<WorkflowBlock>
  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    Draft

    Write your content in the editor. Changes are saved automatically as a draft.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    Review

    Share the draft link with your team and collect feedback inline.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>

  <WorkflowBlockItem>
    Publish

    Push the draft to Main and it goes live on your public docs site.
  </WorkflowBlockItem>
</WorkflowBlock>
